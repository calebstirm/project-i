WHAT WE DO
Christie and Chris' Cooking Cabin is the one-stop source for all of your amateur cooking needs. Our extensive list of products and services that we offer include:
  • Pizza
  • Pasta
  • Burgers
  • Meal Kit Delivery (like HelloFresh)
  • Cooking Tutorials

OUR HISTORY
Our company was founded in 2012 by Christie and Chris Smith. Originally starting out as a small YouTube channel where the couple posted weekly recipe tutorials, the venture has expanded into one of the most popular resources for those stepping in to the world of culinary arts. In the 10 years since Christie and Chris' Cooking Cabin's founding, the company has achieved:
  1. 1,000,000 YouTube Subscribers
  2. 1,000,000 Meal Kits Sold
  3. Multiple Talk Show Apperances by Christie and Chris
